Udon

Ingredients

  • Udon noodles, 1 frozen package
  • Sugar, 1 tsp
  • Dashi, 2 & 1/3s cups
  • Mirin, 1 tbsp
  • Soy Sauce, 1 & 1/2s tbsp
  • Kosher Salt, to taste
  • Green Onions, for garnish

Ingredients - Dashi

  • Water, 4 cups
  • Kombu, 1 piece

Instructions

Making the Dashi

  1. Wipe the kombu with a damp cloth, leaving all white powder intact - it is the source of the umami flavour. cut slits into the kombu for a better release of flavour.
  2. Put the 4 cups of water and the kombu into a pot. Steep for 30 minutes.
  3. Heat over medium-low for around 10 minutes.
  4. Remove the scum using a mesh skimmer.
  5. When the dashi is at a low simmer, remove the kombu from the broth. It is done.

The dashi will keep for 4-5 days in the refrigerator, or 2 weeks if frozen.

Making the Udon

  1. Add the dashi, mirin, sugar, soy sauce, and salt into the pot.
  2. Heat the broth to a low simmer. After simmering, remove the pot from heat and set aside.
  3. To cook the noodles, bring a pot of water to a rolling boil. When the water is boiling, add the noodles and cook according to package instructions.
  4. When the noodles are finished, scoop the noodles out with a mesh strainer or use a colander to drain them.
  5. Serve the noodles in individual bowls and pour the hot broth over them.
  6. Optionally, garnish with the green onion and add more salt, to taste.
